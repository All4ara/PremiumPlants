Medical Card Details

How it Works

Step 1

Visit a Qualified Doctor

Schedule an appointment with a physician that has completed the training required for ordering medical cannabis for patients. View a list of preferred physicians. Your doctor will enter your personal information and dosage recommendation into the Medical Marijuana Use Registry, where it will be saved and stored for the duration of your treatment.

Step 2

Apply for ID Card

All medical marijuana patients and caregivers must have a Medical Marijuana Use Identification Card to receive their order. Visit the Office of Medical Marijuana Use to apply.

Step 3

Fill Your Order

Visit a Premium location near you or call us at 844.PREMIUM and we will fill your order based on the recommended dosage information from your treating physician. You must have with you: 1. Identification with your full name and date of birth; 2. Your state-issued Medical Marijuana Use Identification Card or your approval email from the Office of Medical Marijuana Use.
